Three Priority Revenue Levers

🔴 Lever 1 — Checkout Experience

At 86.3% checkout abandonment, this is the #1 revenue leak. Financing trust, delivery clarity, and payment options are the primary blockers. Every test that increases checkout trust has compounding impact.

🟠 Lever 2 — Hero to Cart Pipeline

Only 1.24% of visitors add to cart. Mobile is 95% of traffic but the hero section and navigation are not optimized for thumb interaction. Wayfair and Home Centre achieve 4–6% ATC on mobile.

🟡 Lever 3 — Value Proposition Clarity

Traffic surged 58% YTD but CR dropped 41%. New visitors from TikTok/Snapchat (2M+ sessions, near-zero revenue) don't understand Al Rugaib's differentiators: 65+ year heritage, Elevarre, design services, 0% financing.

💰 Conservative estimate: if these 30 tests achieve a combined +0.04% CR lift, at 600K monthly sessions and AOV SAR 2,282 → +SAR 547,000 per month / +SAR 6.6M per year in recovered revenue
