How the A/B test works

Variant A (control) keeps the current homepage with mega-menu navigation only. Variant B adds a native Shopify "Collection List" section titled "Shop by Room" in position 3 — below the hero and trust strip. No development required; configured entirely in the Shopify Theme Editor. Test runs 14 days minimum, targeting 95% statistical confidence.

Primary KPI: Collection page CTR from homepage
Secondary: Bounce rate, scroll depth, ATC rate

Why this is our highest-confidence test

Every direct competitor — Home Centre KSA, Abyat, Wayfair — uses a visual category grid. It is the industry standard for furniture e-commerce discovery on mobile. Al Rugaib is the only major KSA furniture retailer without one. The gap is structural, not cosmetic. At 95% mobile traffic with 2M+ social visitors monthly, the absence of visual navigation is directly correlated with our 98.8% no-cart rate.

Confidence level: 92% · Implementation: 2 hours · Cost: SAR 0
